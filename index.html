About

Yutaro Kashiwa is an assistant professor at NAIST (Nara Institute of Science and Technology). I was a post-doc under SENSOR (SENsible SOftware Refactoring) project lead by Yasutaka Kamei and Gabriele Bavota. My Ph.D degree has been given from Wakayama University, supervised by Masao Ohira.

I am working on Mining Software Repository studies. My research interests include software tests and execution traces generated during test exercises.

I am always welcome to collaborate with or join in my team. Feel free to contact me.
